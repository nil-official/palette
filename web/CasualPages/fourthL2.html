How to watch Palette on your TV

To watch Palette on your TV, you need a device with the Palette app. The Palette app is available on many:

Smart TVs

Set-top boxes

Blu-ray players

Game consoles

On Palette-ready TVs, the Palette app is on the main menu or home screen. If your TV has an app store, search for Palette to see if the app is available.

NOTE: Sometimes a device update is needed to find and install the Palette app. If you're not sure how to update the system software on your TV, check your owner's manual or contact the manufacturer. On TVs that don't have the app, you can use a streaming media player or a mobile device to watch Palette on a TV.
