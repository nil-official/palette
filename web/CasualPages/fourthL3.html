How to download titles to watch offline

Find and download a TV show or movie
Tap on any TV show or movie and look for the Downloads icon downward facing arrow to see whether it can be downloaded, or filter by what is available for download. Not all titles are available for download.
Open the Palette app and tap Downloads downward facing arrow.

On a device running Windows 10 or Windows 11, open the Palette app and select Menu .

Select See What You Can Download, Find Something to Download, Find More to Download, or Available for Download, depending on your device.

Select a TV show or movie.

From the description page, tap Download downward facing arrow.

For TV shows, Download downward facing arrow will appear next to each available episode. Android users will also see a Download Season option to download all episodes in the selected season.
To save on data usage, download over Wi-Fi.
You can have up to 100 active downloads at a time per device on as many devices included in your membership plan.
If you cancel your account, TV shows and movies youâ€™ve downloaded will be deleted from your device. You will need to download them again if you rejoin in the future.
Watch downloaded TV shows and movies
To make sure you always have access to your downloads, we recommend remaining signed in to the Palette app.
Sign in to the Palette app and select Downloads downward facing arrow.
On a device running Windows 10 or Windows 11, open the Palette app and select Menu menu icon, then My Downloads.
Find the title you want to watch and select Play play button .
For TV shows, select a show, then tap Play play button next to the episode you want to watch.
